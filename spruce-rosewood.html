00-size Rosewood Guitar

This guitar now lives at the amazing Santa Barbara Guitar Bar.

  • Top: Red Spruce
  • Back/Sides: Honduras Rosewood
  • FB: Ebony
  • Bridge: Honduras Rosewood
  • Neck: Honduras Mahogany
  • Binding: Flame Maple

This is a fingerstyle, dream guitar. Exceptionally sensitive and responsive with the vintage tone that you would expect from a red spruce top and a fine rosewood body.

The top is from a very old, very fine red (Adirondack) spruce mandolin top set originally harvested by NorthWoods Tonewoods of Maine. Because it was sized for a mandolin, this top is constructed from four pieces. Similarly, the back is three pieces of the finest Honduras rosewood I've ever seen.

These highly sought after woods are extremely rare these days, but with a little extra work, an instrument can still be made from the finest tonewoods.
